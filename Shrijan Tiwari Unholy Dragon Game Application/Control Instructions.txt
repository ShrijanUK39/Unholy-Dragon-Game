Controls Instructions
Select - Press Enter 
Pause - Press Escape 
Move Right - Press Right Arrow
Move Left - Press Left Arrow 
Jumping - Press W
Sprinting - Press E
Fireball Spell - Press R
Dash Attack - Press F
Fireball Spell Attack Up - Press Up Arrow + R
Double Jump - Press W twice 
Sprint Jump - Press Right Arrow + E + W
Jump Dash Attack - Press W + F
Sprint Jump Dash Attack- Press Right Arrow + E then W and F or Press Left Arrow + E then W and F.
